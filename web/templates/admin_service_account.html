{# ── How it works ── #}

Setup Instructions

  1. Log in to EVE with a director-level character (corp or alliance executor director).
  2. Click Register Director Character and authorise via EVE SSO.
  3. Click Sync Now — the system will pull all corp/alliance contacts and cache their standings.
  4. From now on, any applicant whose character, corp, or alliance has a negative standing will be automatically flagged in their review report.

Required ESI scopes: esi-corporations.read_contacts.v1 and esi-alliances.read_contacts.v1
